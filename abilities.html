Sharingan - Itachi's most famous technique is his Sharingan, a rare and powerful doujutsu (eye technique) that allows him to copy and memorize any jutsu he sees, as well as read and predict his opponent's movements. Tsukuyomi - Itachi's genjutsu (illusion technique) is Tsukuyomi, which allows him to trap his opponent in a nightmarish world of his own creation, where time seems to slow down and every moment feels like an eternity.

Kotoamatsukami - Itachi can implant a suggestion in the mind of his target, forcing them to follow his orders without question. Demonic Illusion: Mirror Heaven and Earth Change - Itachi can create a mirror that reflects his opponent's movements and actions, allowing him to predict and counter their attacks. Demonic Illusion: Shackling Stakes - Itachi can create illusionary stakes that pin his opponent to the ground, immobilizing them.

イタチ

Itachi is also a skilled hand-to-hand combatant, and can use his speed and agility to strike his opponents with lightning-fast kicks and punches. Itachi's nature transformations included Fire, Water, Wind, Yin, and Yang Release. As an Uchiha, he had a natural affinity for Fire Release, mastering the clan's Great Fireball Technique at a very young age. He could coat shuriken with fire to increase their offensive capacities.

Shurikenjutsu - Itachi is a skilled user of shurikenjutsu, and can throw shuriken and other projectiles with incredible accuracy and speed. Kunai - Itachi is skilled with kunai, using them as both a ranged and melee weapon. Tantō - Itachi is also proficient with the tantō, a short sword.

Itachi's Mangekyō Sharingan - Itachi's Mangekyō Sharingan has unique abilities, including: Amaterasu - Itachi's most powerful jutsu is Amaterasu, a black flame that is said to be the hottest and most destructive fire in existence.
